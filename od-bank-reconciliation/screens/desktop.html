Save yourself from clicking mark paid 50 times a month. Drop in a statement, confirm the matches Henry pre-checked, and the paid pile flips in one click.

What this is (and isn't)
  • Is A payment shortcut — matches statement lines to invoices, costs, and bills already in HeyHenry, then bulk-marks them paid.
  • Isn't Bank reconciliation — your book of record stays in QuickBooks. Your bookkeeper reconciles there.
  • Skips Transfers, fees, interest, ATM withdrawals — those belong in QBO and are hidden by default.

The matcher is the leverage here. Deterministic — amount (50) + date (30) + payee text (20). ≥85 = high (pre-checked), 60–84 = medium, 30–59 = low, <30 = unmatched (QBO's pile). Henry proposes; the human commits. Money flips only on click. e-Transfer / cheque / Stripe / cash all reconcile the same way — amount + date + payee, not payment rail. Bookkeepers reconcile against QBO's bank feed; we don't pretend to be the book of record.
