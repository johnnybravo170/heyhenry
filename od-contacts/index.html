Brief alignment

  • One directory, seven kinds — Lead · Customer · Vendor · Sub-trade · Inspector · Referral partner · Other. No "Agent" (de-scoped for GC).
  • Lead vs. Customer is project-derived — surfaced via the Signal column: leads show "New · Nd ago" or "No project yet · Nd"; customers show "N active · $X due" (CAD tabular numerals, de-emphasized cents, $ figure owner/admin-only).
  • Kind labels are calm — neutral muted pill for everything except Lead, which gets the warn-soft tone because it implies "act on me." Customer subtype shows as a quiet mono eyebrow under the pill ("Residential"), not a second color. No 8-color rainbow.
  • Henry duplicates banner — soft rust accent, the only colored embedded-intelligence surface on the screen. Flags Home Depot Pro (both a Lead and a Vendor in the mock). Dismissible. Henry is labelled; it is not a chat.
  • CASL cue — quiet mono "Messaging off" line on rows where do_not_auto_message is on (Daniel & Priya Mohan in the mock). Faint, not loud.
  • Color budget — rust is reserved for the duplicates banner accent + the org avatar + the inbox count. The primary New contact CTA is ink-fill, not rust. Three type sizes on screen: 16 (names) / 14 (body) / 12 (labels, dates, kind sub).
  • Pagination footer — 1–10 of 24 with page chrome; replaces the old 200-row hard cap.
Notes: the search bar is the universal entry point ("name, email, phone, or city"); kind chips are an additional filter, not a sort. The empty-state and the import flow are out of scope for this pass (brief lists them as secondary).
