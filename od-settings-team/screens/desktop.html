Applies to everyone — override per person above.
Workers can log expenses
Field crew can capture receipts from the truck. Subs and employees both, unless blocked per-person.
Workers can submit invoices
Typical for subcontractors. Off by default for employees.
Workers can edit time entries older than 48 hours
Entries within 48 hours of logging are always editable by the worker. Turn this on to let them backfill or correct older entries too.
Auto-assign crew to new projects
Every active worker is added to a project when it's created. You can still remove individuals after the fact.
