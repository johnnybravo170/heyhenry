Project summary

What we built together

Over four months we transformed Helen's galley kitchen into an open, light-filled space anchored by a nine-foot maple island. The work included a full structural opening between kitchen and dining, new electrical and plumbing service to the island, custom shaker cabinetry painted in Benjamin Moore White Dove, and a Caesarstone Pure White countertop with a full-height handmade-tile backsplash.

Two change orders — adding island pendant lighting and upgrading the range hood for stronger ventilation — were approved partway through and finished in step. The project wrapped on May 15, on schedule with the two-day allowance added by the hood upgrade.
