Why promote, not merge

  • Idea Board is client inspiration. Selections is the decided spec. Different objects, different lifecycles.
  • Promotion is one-way: the idea gets a "promoted" stamp; the selection becomes the source of truth.
  • The idea stays visible — useful context — but you can't edit a Selection by editing an idea.
  • Image and notes are inherited; brand · code · finish · supplier · SKU need your operator-grade fill-in.
  • by-promoted tag is rendered everywhere the promoted Selection appears — operator AND client portal.
